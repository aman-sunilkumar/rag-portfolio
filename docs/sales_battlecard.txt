Sales Battlecard: Competitive Positioning Q1 2025
Author: Sales Enablement | Team: Sales | March 2025

Product Overview
Our platform delivers AI-powered workflow automation that reduces manual work by 60-80%.
Key differentiators: fast deployment, no-code customization, enterprise security compliance.

Against Competitor A
Their weakness: long implementation timelines averaging 6-9 months.
Our advantage: 2-week onboarding, 30-40% lower TCO over 3 years.
Objection: "We already use Competitor A" -> Ask about their hidden implementation costs.

Against Competitor B
Their weakness: limited integration ecosystem with fewer than 50 native connectors.
Our advantage: 200+ connectors, support and training included at no extra cost.
Objection: "Competitor B is good enough" -> Use ROI calculator with their actual numbers.

Key Proof Points
- Customer A: 74% reduction in manual processing time, ROI in 4 months.
- Customer B: 3x improvement in analyst productivity, $2.1M annual savings.
- Customer C: Enterprise-wide deployment in 6 weeks with zero downtime.

Discovery Questions
1. What does your current process look like for this use case?
2. How much time does your team spend on this manual task per week?
3. What would a 70% reduction in that time mean for your team?
4. Who else is involved in the purchasing decision?

Pricing Guidance
Standard: $X/seat/month, minimum 20 seats.
Enterprise: Custom pricing with SLA, dedicated CSM, SSO included.
Do not discount more than 20% without VP approval.
Always push for annual upfront to improve unit economics.

Objection: "It is too expensive"
Reframe to TCO and ROI. Use the ROI calculator. Show 12-month payback period.
Escalate to SE for a technical deep dive if the deal requires it.
