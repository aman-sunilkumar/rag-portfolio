Product Requirements Document: Onboarding V2
Author: Growth PM Team | Team: Growth | Q1 2025

Executive Summary
The Onboarding V2 initiative reduces time-to-value for new users from 14 days to 7 days.
Current activation rate at day 7 is 42%. Target is 65% by end of Q2 2025.

Problem Statement
User research reveals three primary drop-off points:
1. Account setup (23% drop-off): Too many required fields before value is shown.
2. First action (31% drop-off): Users don't understand what to do first.
3. Aha moment (19% drop-off): Users complete setup but don't hit the key value event.

Proposed Solution
Phase 1: Progressive onboarding - show value before requiring full account setup.
Phase 2: Contextual tooltips and guided first actions based on user role.
Phase 3: Personalized aha moment triggers based on user segment.

Success Metrics
- Day 7 activation rate: 42% -> 65%
- Time to first key action: 3.2 days -> 1.5 days
- 30-day retention: 58% -> 71%

Timeline
Week 1-2: UX research and design
Week 3-4: Engineering build
Week 5: A/B test launch
Week 6-8: Measure and iterate

Risk and Mitigations
Risk: Engineering capacity conflict with platform migration.
Mitigation: Phased rollout with feature flags to reduce scope if needed.

Recommendation
Prioritize Onboarding V2 as Q1 top initiative. Allocate 2 engineers and 1 designer.
Expected ROI: 23% improvement in paid conversion based on cohort modeling.
